NJKeyClub

Website

From my Junior-Senior year I was a part of the New Jersey District Board of Key Club, serving as their webmaster. Besides a plethora of other responsibilities I managed and updated the District Website. A lot of the content on the website was pre-existing, however I revamped many of the pages making them viable for a digital school year (Due to complications with Covid-19).

Instead of the bland proffesional feeling that the website gave before my term, my goal whiile in office was to make the website more vibrant and welcoming to members who wanted to learn more about the District.

My involvement includes: updating information on board members, creating the member recognition section, modifying the RTCs and District Project pages, and changing general information and dates.
