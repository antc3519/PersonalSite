Faraday Motors

Website

During the school year of 2020, my friends and I were encuraged to work on a project in our TSA club being members of the STEM program. Originally I was working on the Video Game Design competition however that event got cancelled due to virtualization so I switched over to Wemaster.

The theme for 2020 was to design an environmentally friendly automotive company's website, which for us was Faraday Motors. The website we made included: 3 car model pages, inventory, about us, support, news, and contacts.

My involvement includes: Designing Home and Inventory Pages, coding and hosting all pages of the website, assisting with the design of other pages, documenting progress.
